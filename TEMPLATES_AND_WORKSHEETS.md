## VALIDATION SPREADSHEET TEMPLATE

Use this to score product ideas before building.

```
PRODUCT IDEA VALIDATION MATRIX

Product Idea: [Name]
Niche: [Category]
Target Customer: [Who]
Date Validated: [Date]

SCORING (0-10 scale):

SIGNAL 1: SEARCH VOLUME
Question: How many monthly searches for this keyword?
Score: ___ /10
(8+ = 500+ searches, 5-7 = 100-500, 0-4 = <100)

SIGNAL 2: COMPETITION DENSITY  
Question: How many listings exist for this?
Score: ___ /10
(9-10 = <50 listings, 6-8 = 50-150, 3-5 = 150-300, 0-2 = 300+)

SIGNAL 3: BUYER URGENCY
Question: Do reviews show people NEED this NOW?
Score: ___ /10
(9-10 = High urgency evident, 5-8 = Moderate, 0-4 = Low)

SIGNAL 4: REPEAT PURCHASE POTENTIAL
Question: Can someone buy this multiple times?
Score: ___ /10
(9-10 = High (template they customize 5+ times), 5-8 = Medium, 0-4 = Low)

SIGNAL 5: EXPANDABILITY
Question: Can this become 3-5 variations?
Score: ___ /10
(9-10 = Yes easily, 5-8 = Maybe, 0-4 = Probably not)

SIGNAL 6: PRICE ELASTICITY
Question: Can you price this $15-$49?
Score: ___ /10
(9-10 = Yes with room to test, 5-8 = Narrow range, 0-4 = Can't price competitively)

---

TOTAL SCORE: _____ /60

WEIGHTED AVERAGE: _____ /10
(Score 1×0.3 + Score 2×0.2 + Score 3×0.15 + Score 4×0.15 + Score 5×0.1 + Score 6×0.1)

---

DECISION:
☐ 8-10: BUILD IMMEDIATELY
☐ 6-8: BUILD WITH MODIFICATIONS
☐ 4-6: RESEARCH MORE BEFORE DECIDING
☐ 0-4: KILL IDEA, MOVE ON

NOTES:
[Write specific reasons for your decision]

---

VALIDATION FOLLOW-UP:
After product launches, return here and note actual performance:

Launch date: ______
First week impressions: ______
First week clicks: ______
First week sales: ______
Conversion rate: ______% (Sales ÷ Clicks)

Did actual performance match prediction? ☐ Yes ☐ Somewhat ☐ No

What would you score differently now?
[Space for reflection]
```
