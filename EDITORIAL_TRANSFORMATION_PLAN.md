### 7. WRITING STYLE IMPROVEMENTS

#### Issues to Address
- Avoid: "You can do it!" motivational tone
- Avoid: Repeated emphasis statements
- Avoid: Generic inspirational framing
- Add: Analytical depth
- Add: Operational precision
- Add: Evidence-based reasoning
- Add: Conditional language
- Add: Risk acknowledgment

#### Style Guide for Revision

```
REPLACE THIS          →    WITH THIS
"easily doubles"            "typically increases by 1.5-2x under 
                            these conditions:"

"You need to..."            "The process requires..."

"Guaranteed"                "Likely, assuming proper execution"

"anyone can"                "creators with existing design or 
                            writing background typically..."

"amazing results"           "measurable improvements in CTR 
                            from [X]% to [Y]%"

"game changer"              "significant operational 
                            improvement for..."

"just follow"               "systematic implementation of..."
```
